Documents for the frontier.

Workpads are documents that can be shared & viewed in browsers or apps - without passing through the Internet.

Document content is encoded & compressed into strings, creating shareable links. Each Workpad displays content locally using templates and can be easily secured using encrypted keys.

-----

Workpads is based on the 'Business Assistance System' (BASICS) being developed by Babb, its global partners & volunteers.
