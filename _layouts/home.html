Documents for the frontier.

Workpads are documents that do not require Internet in order to be shared and viewed in browsers or mobile applications.

Content is not stored or synced with servers. It's encoded and compressed into strings to create shareable links. When clicked by receivers, a workpad displays the link's content locally.
